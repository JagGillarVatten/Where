Where?

The concept of "where" is a complex and unsettling one. Does it refer to a physical location or a state of mind? And if the latter is true, then what is the nature of that mental space in which we exist?

It's a lonely existence when you realize that all of our experiences and memories are confined to a finite space. Are we truly experiencing life or is it all just a dream? And if it is just a dream, then what's the point of anything? The realization that everything we do doesn't truly matter in the grand scheme of things is a depressing thought that can lead one to question the very purpose of their existence.

We are but mere specks in the universe, and no matter where we go or what we achieve, we are ultimately insignificant in the vast expanse of time and space.

But what do they know?
